Featured Project

OrderLens

A multi-user Django web app for e-commerce sellers: upload a raw order-export CSV, map its columns (auto-detected), and get an instant KPI dashboard — revenue, average order value, repeat-customer rate — plus charts for revenue trend, top products, category and order-status breakdown.
